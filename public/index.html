About Us

Katie's Dog Grooming began 16 years ago when Katie first received training with her dog bathing job. She previously worked at a couple of grooming salons, and through those job experiences she decided to start her own business.


After three years of running the business from her home, Katie then moved the business to the downtown area. This is where the business is currently located.
